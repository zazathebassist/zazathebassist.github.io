Yearly Theme 2022: Year of Creation

2022-01-10 :: emma celeste :: 3 min read (577 words)

It’s that time of year again. Yearly Theme time. And I want to coast off the success of last year’s theme. Last year I set myself on the path of the Year of Discovery. And through that, I explored more of California and of myself then I have in a long time. I am content that I marked a good path that will allow me to keep moving forward and explore myself and the world around me more. But I want to do more than just see new things. I want to create. I have this need in me not just to observe the beauty of this world, but to contribute to it.

That is why this year I am setting off on the Year of Creation.

Creativity has been something I have struggled with regularly throughout my life. I have regularly wanted to or tried to pursue creative venues, but either because of some bad executive dysfunction, overly busy schedules, or imposter syndrome I have ended up not following through. My most creative solo venture, my TikTok page, lasted essentially 4 months before I needed to take a break. I’m not happy about that.

However, I feel like I have entered a new part of my life. One where I am able to dedicate the time and energy to create in the ways I want to, and I want a theme that will help me pursue this better. But the big thing here is what I mean by “creativity”.

And honestly, I’m keeping it pretty open and vague for a reason. I have a lot of creative outlets and would rather just set myself the goal of creating something beautiful instead of trying to lock it down to something more specific like “write a song a month” or “upload a TikTok a week”. This is more about building a habit of making things than it is the actual output.

And to add to that, a big part of this is going to be not caring about the quality of my finished product. I am not trying to make an album that will chart or another TikTok that breaks 100k views. I’m just trying to be me, and create things that I like.

The creative venues I have available to me are… too many. But I would like to spend more time creating music and video content. Above all, I always come back to music and video as the two creative venues that fill me with the most joy and offset that difficulty I have in creating stuff. I don’t like setting myself goals, but I want to have music published. I have run a semi-successful video channel already, music is the one venue I have not truly been able to just make my own. It has always been tied to other people. And with my band in a difficult spot, this year more than ever will be a good year for this.

So, here I go. This article is part of the Year of Creation. I got two blog posts out in a week. I don’t expect to keep this pace, but I wanna keep this energy through the year.

The concept of yearly themes comes from the podcast Cortex with CGP Grey and Myke Hurley. They have genuinely helped me think of my life in a way better way. You can also check them out a video that Grey made about yearly themes here https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NVGuFdX5guE
